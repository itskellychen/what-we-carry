Laptop Dongles

Jose, 20

Small Grey Metal

DESCRIPTION

A USB-C dongle with ports to USB-A, USB-C, and HDMI connections. Perfect for those newer devices that only seem to have USB-C ports.

SENTIMENTALS

I'd say the only sentimental attachment I have to this item is the reason why I had to buy it. Recently, I had the privilege of investing in a new laptop for the purpose of school. The only downside, however, is that it the computer only had USB-C ports. I had to buy an adapter to fit the rest of my needs regarding USB-A connections. It was really cool to buy a new piece of technology for my other new piece of technology.

PRACTICALITY

These are absolutely useful. Without it I would only be half as productive.

OTHER

On a scale of 1-5, how proud are you to own this item?

Cost of item: More than $5. I bought it back home in Costa Rica, so I'm not too sure about its availability.
