Random Receipts

Kelly, 19

Paper Purchases Recycle

DESCRIPTION

These random receipts are proof of my past expenses. Though a bit random, they're good to have around.

SENTIMENTALS

There's something great about getting handed a physical, tangible receipt after every purchase you make. Though I wouldn't consider it an "essential" carry, I still carry these random receipts wherever I go. I enjoy looking back on them every now and then.

PRACTICALITY

Want to return something and need to provide proof of purchase? Want to keep track of your spending habits? It turns out that having these random receipts around can be more practical than you think.
