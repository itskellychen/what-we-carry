Baggu Tote Bag

Bonnie, 30

Versatile Easy Waterproof

DESCRIPTION

You can fit almost anything in this tote bag because it is spacious and foldable. It can be used for work, school, shopping, and more. It is one of the few bags that is versatile with many options for usage. It is also eco-friendly!

SENTIMENTALS

The tote bag is a collaboration between Baggu and J.Crew. It was purchased at J.Crew's first retail store in Toronto.

PRACTICALITY

Yes, this item helps me on a daily basis and I always keep the tote bag (or any type of foldable tote bag) in my backpack or purse. It is useful when I go shopping or if I unexpectedly have things to carry.
