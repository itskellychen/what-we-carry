White Rabbit Candy

Kelly, 19

Snack Wrapper Nostalgic

DESCRIPTION

The perfect, quick on-the-go snack without having to deal with any mess. Especially useful when you're craving something milky that's not heavy afterwards.

SENTIMENTALS

The white rabbit candy was a recent addition to my everyday carries. My mom bought a big bag of them to celebrate Chinese New Year, and it's become a bit of a tradition. I always look forward to eating them each year.

PRACTICALITY

Ironically, as much as I love this candy, I often forget I have one in my bag. So, they sit in my bag uneaten for the most part. But it does cheer me whenever I remember to eat them.
