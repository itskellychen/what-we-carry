Floss Container

Kelly, 19

Teeth Green Plastic

DESCRIPTION

Everyone can benefit from carrying around some floss. You never know when you might need it.

SENTIMENTALS

I started carrying around this floss container from a somewhat embarrassing experience I shared with my friends last summer. We had just finished eating takoyaki at Kensington Market, and I had some stuck on my two front teeth. Unfortunately, I didn't have any floss on me and struggled to get it out for what felt like the longest time. I avoided talking (and showing my teeth) until I finally got it off.

I told my mom about this experience afterwards, and so she gave me this floss container. Now I carry it everywhere I go.

PRACTICALITY

My story above confirms its practicality. It's useful for quickly touching up.
